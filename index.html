About PassOnBits

PassOnBits was created with a simple mission: to reduce waste and build stronger campus communities by making it easy for students to share items they no longer need.

We believe that every item deserves a second chance, and every student deserves access to affordable resources. Our platform connects students across campuses, fostering a culture of sharing, sustainability, and community support.
